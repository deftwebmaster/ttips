Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Period.

TadaTips doesn’t collect personal information. We don’t track you, fingerprint you, or sell your data to shady ad networks. We’re not interested in surveillance — we’re interested in sharing good tips.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics (like Plausible or Umami) to understand general traffic trends. These tools don’t use cookies, don’t collect IP addresses, and don’t follow you around the web.

Cookies

This site doesn’t use cookies for advertising or user tracking. If any cookies are set in the future, they’ll be used only to improve your experience (like remembering preferences) — and we’ll let you know.

Third-Party Links

Some of our tips may include affiliate links to tools or products we genuinely recommend. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission. It won’t cost you anything extra.

Your Data

You’re not required to provide any personal data to use TadaTips. If we ever add an email list or submission form, it’ll be optional, transparent, and easy to unsubscribe or delete your info.

Changes

This privacy policy may evolve as the site grows. If we make any significant changes, we’ll post them right here — no fine print or sneaky moves.
